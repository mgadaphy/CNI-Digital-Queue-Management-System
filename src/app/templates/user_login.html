How to Get an Enrollment Code
Online Pre-Registration

Visit the CNI official website and complete the online pre-registration form. You'll receive an enrollment code via email or SMS.

Visit CNI Office

Go to any CNI office with your required documents to complete pre-registration and receive your enrollment code.

Call CNI Hotline

Call the CNI customer service hotline for assistance with pre-registration and enrollment code generation.

Important: You must complete pre-registration before visiting the office to avoid long wait times.
